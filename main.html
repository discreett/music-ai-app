What is TunedTutor?

Guitar tuner is a AI-powered tool that assists musicians, in achieving their musical goals. It provides real-time feedback on the tuning of your guitar, helping you to ensure that your instrument is always in perfect pitch. TunedTutor utilizes advanced AI algorithms to track finger positions and provides feedback based on your playing style. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, this tool is designed to enhance your musical experience.
